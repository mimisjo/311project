Chicago 311 Service Request Analysis

Team Members

  • Keith Greenwalt
  • Ryan Kingsbury
  • Miriam Sjoblom
  • Allan Sun

Description

We examined the amount of time it took for the city to respond to service requests to see if there were differences in how quickly services were closed across Chicago's 50 wards. We also looked at estimates of the mean household income for each ward to see if there was a difference in response times between wards that corresponded with income levels.
